Juan Pavez, Ph.D.(c)

Juan Pavez is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María. Juan also holds a Master Degree in Informatics from Universidad Santa María. His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing and Probability Theory. He has more than 5 years of experience working on applied data science projects, especially in the field of High Energy Physics. He also has experience working at big scientific collaboration such as the Large Hadron Collider where he did an internship as a Ph.D. student.

His research includes work in the intersection of Machine Learning and High Energy Physics, work in Deep Learning applied to Natural Language processing and work in the theory of Machine Learning. As a software Developer Juan has large and demonstrated of experience working in Python and C++. This includes teaching courses of C++ at the UTFSM.
