Sebastián Rodríguez, BCSc

Sebastián Rodríguez holds a Bachelor of Computer Science from the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso.

Since 2016, Sebastián has worked on a number of different research projects as both a project engineer and research assistant. These projects have included areas such as machine learning, natural language processing, and data visualization. Regarding software development, Sebastián has experience in building and deploying machine learning-based solutions in Python. For web development, Sebastián employs Python with HTML5 JavaScript-based libraries.

Sebastián is currently an associated researcher for WriteWise, where he is applying data science and natural language processing methodologies to assess academic articles. His investigative goals are to analyze, describe, and predict the relevant characteristics of articles using traditional machine learning and deep learning.
