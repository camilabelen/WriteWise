OUR QUESTION

Established in mid-2017 our group address fundamental questions on scientific writing using cutting-edge technology.

How we can help scientist to write and communicate better their research? What is the difference between a properly and poorly written paper?

What makes an easy-to-read and logic-flow written paper? What is the linguistic behind this?

How we can help editorials to improve and speed up review process through automatization?

How we make scientific writing review less bias and more objective? Is it possible to quantify a paper based on its writing?

OUR R&D

We are starting to answer these questions through state-of-the art AI techniques, and new models, functions, and algorithms developed by our group. Through applied research our main purpose is to develop new technology; thus, modernizing and improving scientific writing.
