ABOUT US

Founded in 2017 as a BioPub spin-off, WriteWise is the first company to apply Artificial Intelligence towards providing specialized, in-depth feedback on the writing, editing, and publishing processes for scientific articles. Without doubt, WriteWise will be a valuable tool for students, researchers, and academic publishers worldwide.

In less than one year, WriteWise has accomplished several key milestones, including:

COLLABORATION NETWORK

Establish a robust collaboration network with experienced entrepreneurs, linguists, and researchers in education, AI, NLP, and deep learning.

FUNDING

Raise funding and receive support from prestigious international and national institutions (Microsoft, MIT, CORFO).

PROTOTYPING

Begin prototyping, with user-testing planned for the near future.

EXPAND TEAM

Exponentially expand the WriteWise team, which today includes 12 members, 8 of whom hold a PhD.

ENTER THE MARKET

Lay the foundations for market entry, with sales expected as soon as 2018.
