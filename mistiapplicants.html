Transforming the Process of Writing Academic Papers with Artificial Intelligence

In partnership with the MIT International Science and Technology Initiatives (MISTI), we invite motivated MIT students with experience in the computer sciences to complete an internship with our multidisciplinary team, which is currently dedicated to redefining how scientific documents are written.

THE BENEFITS OF INTERNING WITH

Form part of a significant project with global impacts

Live the experience of working in a technological startup in Chile (different than the US) with a multidisciplinary team

Creative, independent work oriented towards personal interests

Flexible hours

Flexible vacation time

Professional development and growth (Perks), specifically:

  • Gain basic and specific knowledge on scientific writing, as well as on machine learning as applied to text analysis
  • Participate in academic collaborations
  • Be considered an author of publications and conference presentations, depending on contributions
  • Be considered in patent applications, depending on contributions

ABOUT THE INTERNSHIP

Interns will be involve in cutting-edge research in the areas of machine learning and big data analytics. Some of the R+D+i topics that interns may be involved include:

  • Supervised and unsupervised machine learning techniques to organize and classify massive databases of scientific text.

  • NLP models for text representation, classification, and feature selection.

  • Deep learning classifiers for providing journal suggestions.

INTERN PROFILE

We are looking for highly motivated students interested in scientific writing and applying AI to improve the writing process. Interns will be expected to independently approach challenges and regularly interact with WriteWise Team Members and Collaborators. Interns should actively propose novel solutions to the problem WriteWise is addressing and participate in brainstorming sessions. It is critical that interns have an innovative, “start-up” mindset.
