Cristhian Gómez, B.Eng., M.Sc.

Marketing and Commercial Management

  • contact@writewise.cl

Cristhian is an Industrial Engineer (Pontificia Universidad Javeriana, Colombia). He received his Master’s in Marketing from the Universidad Externado (Colombia) and an Associate Degree in Big Data for Public Policies from the Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez (Chile). Cristhian has experience in the public and private sectors, having taken on responsibilities such as project management, strategic planning, and marketing.

Cristhian has experience as an Independent Consultant for Small-to-Mid Sized Businesses in Chile, and has worked in the Economic Development Subsecretary of the Bogota District in Colombia, gaining experience in export commerce, project management, marketing, and strategic planning. Additionally, Cristhian has conducted internships and consulting work related to logistics for Coca-Cola FEMSA (Colombia) and Crawford & Company (Colombia). Cristhian also has experience in subjects related to in-person and e-commerce payment security through consulting work for the Banca en Incocrédito.

Cristhian is currently overseeing the areas of marketing and commercial strategy for WriteWise, investigating applicable approaches of Artificial Intelligence Marketing for the academic and publishing sectors.
