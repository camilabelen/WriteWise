Francisca Schalchli, B.Eng.

Project Manager

Francisca is an Engineer in Product Design (Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Chile). She has experience in business and in research + design methodologies. Francisca has participated in innovation-oriented projects for small and large businesses, drawing on the expertise of multidisciplinary teams to create distinct commercial technologies. Francisca focusses on developing solutions that solve specific client and user issues, and has worked for distinct businesses in the technology sector (Asmar and Carozzi). Francisca also has proven experience in research, having participated in government-funded grants (e.g., Fondecyt, SSAF Corfo) and in projects related to the entrepreneurship ecosystem in Chile (e.g., Jump Chile and Stgo Makerspace).

Francisca is currently a Project Manager at WriteWise, a role in which she has successfully overseen application processes to highly competitive projects, including the business incubator ImagineLab (Microsoft Innovation Center), with WriteWise being 1 of the 48 selected projects.
