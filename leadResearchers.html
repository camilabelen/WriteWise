René Venegas, PhD

Dr. René Venegas holds a PhD in Linguistics and is a Full Professor of Linguistics at the Pontificia Universidad Católica. Dr. Venegas has over 20 years of experience in linguistics, specializing in rhetorical discourse analysis, computational linguistics, and corpus analyses. Currently, Dr. Venegas is leading the Linguistics Research Line for the development of the WriteWise software.

Héctor Allende, PhD

Dr. Héctor Allende holds a PhD in Computer Science from the Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María and is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the Pontificia Universidad Católica. Dr. Allende has over ten years of experience in machine learning, data science, and distributed computing. Currently, Dr. Allende is leading the Machine Learning Research Line for the development of the WriteWise software.

Wenceslao Palma, PhD
